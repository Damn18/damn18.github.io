description: This project was developed during the Data4Econ Hackathon 2025, where participants analyze real-world datasets provided by Alperia and Gruppo Dolomiti Energia. The goal was to predict hourly energy production for January 2025 using historical production data and weather forecasts. After preprocessing and feature selection, several models were tested, including MLP and LSTM. Results show that simpler models combined with feature engineering performed best.
