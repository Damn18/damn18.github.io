description: This project was developed during the Data4Econ Hackathon 2026, hosted by Unibz, sponsored by [Key to Energy](https://keytoenergy.it/en/) under the patronage of y-SIS and the CLAssification and Data Analysis Group. The full-day event focused on forecasting hourly wind energy production by combining 1.5 years of data with weather predictions and evaluating the reliability of the input information. The challenge required the development of predictive models and the delivering of both a numerical forecast and a report explaining the methodology applied.
