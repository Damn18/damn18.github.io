description: This project investigates the determinants of Airbnb prices in Bologna using spatial econometric models. A dataset was constructed from InsideAirbnb sources and enriched with geographic and host-related variables. After testing for spatial autocorrelation with Moran’s I, a Spatial Error Model (SEM) was estimated to account for spatial dependence. The results show that proximity to the city center and host professionalization significantly increase prices, while private/shared rooms and higher review counts are associated with lower prices.
