As a Computer vision engineer Intern, I work on the development and evaluation of deep learning models for fine-grained image analysis tasks, focusing on object detection, segmentation, and classification. My responsibilities include fine-tuning, architecture modification, performance benchmarking, and inference optimization, on YOLO, DETR, SAM, and CLIP-based models for domain-specific use cases in marine imagery.

Moreover, I contribute to the development of a platform for auto-labeling, image labeling and model fine-tuning, intended to support scientists and marine biologists in building and managing computer vision workflows.
