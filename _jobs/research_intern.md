As data science visiting researcher, I built a pipeline on the university's HPC to analyse millions of textual and graph data of the user base of Bluesky, a new social media with a few years of life. Specifically, I analysed how the public discourse over climate change structured and evolved in 1.5 year, from the public release of the social platform until data extraction (02/2024 - 07/2025). The approaches applied align with recent methodological advances in NLP, network science, and CSS.
